Care & Handling Guide

Safety First

To protect both the artwork and your home, please observe these safety precautions:

  • LED Lights Only / No Open Flames: The wooden display, Washi paper, and kimono fabrics are highly flammable. Never use real candles or open flames. Use only the included LED lights.
  • Choking Hazard: This product contains small parts (earrings, lantern components). Please keep it out of reach of small children and pets to prevent accidental ingestion.
  • For Display Only: This is an art piece, not a toy. Please handle it with care, as excessive force may cause damage.

Handling Your Art Piece

Due to its intricate construction, please take care when setting up:

  • Arm Positioning: The arms contain internal wires and can be adjusted slightly. However, they are not designed for frequent movement. Please handle them gently when adjusting the pose.
  • Lantern (Teshoku) Attachment: There is a small hole in the fox's palm. Align the lantern's stem with this hole and insert it gently. Do not force it; find the position where it sits securely.
  • Lighting & Battery: Due to international shipping regulations, the battery is not included. Please purchase a CR927 button cell separately.
  • Note: The LED light unit for the lantern is included with your order.

Maintenance & Longevity

Proper care will allow your piece to age beautifully over time.

  • Cleaning: To remove dust, gently use a soft brush or a camera-style air blower. Avoid wiping with a cloth, as it may damage the delicate Washi paper or small parts.
  • Replacing Shoji Paper: If the Washi paper on the screen is damaged, it can be replaced. Dampen the wooden frame with a wet sponge to peel off the old paper, then apply new paper using shoji glue or starch paste (denpun-nori).
  • Note: Do not use wood glue (PVA), as it will make future replacements impossible.
  • Environment: Avoid direct sunlight and high humidity. These can cause the wood to warp or the fabrics and Washi paper to fade (discolor) over time.

The Nature of Handmade

We embrace the subtle "fluctuations" of handcrafting as the unique character of each piece.

  • The 1mm Variance: Every component is hand-sewn and hand-assembled. Slight variances of about 1mm, visible adhesive marks, or minor asymmetries may occur. These are not defects but a "mark of the hand" - a sign of authentic craftsmanship not found in mass-produced items.
  • Natural Wood Grain: The wooden display stand is made of natural wood. Each piece features a unique grain and color tone. Please enjoy the natural deepening of the wood's color as it ages.
