A Messenger at the Threshold

A fox of folklore, a sanctuary of light - this handcrafted scene captures a quiet fragment of a story. Standing beside a wooden display echoing the geometry of shoji doors and sacred gates, the fox invites you to a peaceful space between the visible and the spiritual. Each piece is a unique creation, designed to bring a touch of Japanese "Yoin" (lingering memory) to your home.

The Art of the Kimono

  • Eba-moyo Layout: The intricate pattern continues seamlessly across the seams, a hallmark of high-quality traditional kimono.
  • Selected Textiles: To find the perfect balance of beauty and durability, I reviewed over 30 different fabrics before choosing this silk-like polyester.
  • Pure Hand-Sewing: Every stitch is made by hand. No glue or shortcuts - only dedicated craftsmanship.

Customization

  • Removable Obi (Sash): While it comes with an original silk-style sash, you can also personalize your fox with your favorite ribbons - perhaps a vintage velvet or a delicate lace.
  • Interchangeable Earrings: Standard fashion earring size (approx. 0.8-1.0 mm / 18-20 Gauge). Includes two pairs of original hand-knotted flower earrings in colors chosen to complement the kimono.
