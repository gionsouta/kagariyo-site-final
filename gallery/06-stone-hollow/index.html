Laughter echoes.
Clear skies.
The scent of a scorching summer.
Grass and earth, still bearing the remnants of the rainy season.

A cool hollow in the stone.

A smile, its ink gently blurred.

A damp white figure crawls forward.
Those shaped by a wish
slip into the depths of the stone.

There,
only the whisper of paper unravelling remains.
