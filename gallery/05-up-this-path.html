Up this path...

Tracing a memory, a haze,
Stepping on half-buried stones, climbing higher,
Beyond the trees...

In an open clearing, small white flowers,
Almost hidden in the grass,
A fallen stone lantern, there again...

And now,
Up much the same stone steps, I go.

On the steps,
An unfamiliar stone guardian...
