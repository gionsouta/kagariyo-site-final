A balmy spring evening.

The whisper of a banquet lingers in the air.

Passersby pause in their tracks,
as small beings begin to gather.

Gazing at the sky, the white being offers a soft smile.

Wisteria rises vividly from the shadows,
feigning to be the night itself.
