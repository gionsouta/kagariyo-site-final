Privacy Policy

At kagariyo we respect your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your personal information.

1. Information We Collect & Use

We collect your name, shipping address, and email address solely for the following purposes:

  • To process and ship your orders.
  • To contact you regarding your purchase or inquiries.
  • To comply with legal and tax obligations.

Note:
All payments are processed by external payment providers. We do not have access to, or store, your full credit card information.

2. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We only share necessary information with trusted third parties to complete your order, such as shipping carriers (Japan Post/EMS) and payment processors.

3. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. If you wish to do so, please contact us via our Contact Form.
